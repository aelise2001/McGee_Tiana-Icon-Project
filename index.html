2023 Writers Strike Casualties

Four shows canceled due to the 2023 writers strike after being greenlit.

By Tiana McGee

October 13, 2023

Credit:Tiana McGee

After lasting for nearly five months, almost no company went unscathed from the fallout of the 2023 Writers Strike. Here are just a few shows that had been greenlit for a second season or set to start production but were canceled due to the strike.

Credit:Tiana McGee

A League of Their Own

This Amazon Prime Video series about a World War II all-female baseball team was based on a 1992 film that starred Rosie O’Donnell but was canceled due to the writers strike.

Credit:Tiana McGee

Pitch Perfect: Bumper in Berlin

This Peacock original series was a spin-off of the hit films in the Pitch Perfect trilogy. It followed Adam Devine as Bumper Allen as he took on Berlin, hoping to revive his music career- renewed for a second season. Canceled due to the writers strike.

Credit:Tiana McGee

Metropolis

This Apple TV+ series was based on the 1927 film by Fritz Lang-canceled in preproduction due to the writers strike.

Credit:Tiana McGee

The Peripheral

This Amazon Prime Video sci-fi series is set in a tech-driven future that stars Chloë Grace Moretz as Flynne Fisher—renewed for a second season. Canceled due to the strike.
